BBQ Ribs

Directions

Ingredients

  • Pork ribs
  • BBQ rub(of your choice)
  • BBQ sauce(of your choice)
  • Dr.Pepper
  • Mayo
  • Hickory or apple smoke pellets
  • Foil

Directions

  1. Remove the membrane from bottom of the ribs
  2. Coat outside of the ribs with mayo
  3. Rub ribs with seasoning of your choice
    • Depending on seasoning you may also add salt, pepper, brown sugar
  4. Preheat grill to 160 degrees F
  5. Place seasoned ribs on grill, smoke for 3 hours
  6. Remove ribs from grill, wrap in foil, pour 3 glogs of Dr.Pepper on ribs, close foil
  7. Raise temperature of grill to 225 degrees F
  8. Grill ribs at 225 for 2 hours.
  9. Remove ribs from foil, apply BBQ sauce of your choice, and place back on grill
  10. Check temperature after 1 hour
  11. Ribs are ready if sauce
  12. Allow ribs to rest for 10 minutes
